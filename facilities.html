Facilities

Facilities at Gymkhana: The Gymkhana is the nodal center for sports and cultural activities for the students and officials of the Institute. Gymkhana has about 35 sports and cultural clubs, which are as under: Aerobics Archery Arts Athletics Badminton Basketball Billiards Carrom Chess Cricket Cycling Dance Dodgeball Drama Fashion show Film Frisbee Football Gym Hockey Handball Kabaddi Kalarippayattu Kho – Kho Kung - Fu Lawn Tennis Literature Music Nature Photography Swimming Table Tennis Volleyball Yoga

Location of Gymkhana facilities: Due to the construction of new indoor sports complex, the Gymkhana facilities are spread across the campus. List of facilities along with their locality is given below. Badminton courts – Beside SBI bank. Billiards & Table Tennis – Hall above ‘A’ mess. Carrom – ‘B’ block TV room. Athletics, Cricket, Football, Hockey, Frisbee, Kung Fu and Lawn Tennis – Gymkhana. Aerobics – Jindal mess 2nd floor. Gymnasium (1), Dance & Yoga – Janata Bazar 2nd floor. Gymnasium (2) – New Boys Hostel (NBH). Swimming pool – Madhura Marg (towards MGH) Basketball, Volleyball, Cricket, Handball, Kho – Kho, Kabaddi etc – KV IISc ground. Cricket practice nets – Ground opposite to SBI bank.
