Sports Grounds

CRICKET GROUND • Day & Night facilities. • Being used by both leather ball and tennis ball cricket users. • All Mondays and Tuesdays will be reserved for maintenance.

TENNIS COURT Proposed new Synthetic Tennis court (Construction is under progress) Synthetic Tennis court presently available for utilization. • Gymkhana has one synthetic court (second one being created) • lighting facilities available • Users will not be allowed to play if the courts are wet due to rain. • All the users must enter the court with proper non-marking tennis shoes.

FOOTBALL GROUND • The new turf ground was inaugurated at Gymkhana on 12th June 2024. This astro turf ground has also got a FIFA certification. • flood lights are provided for the ground. • Frisbee is also permitted to be played on this ground. • Washrooms and changing rooms are available to the users. • This is a non-irrigated turf, and users are advised to wear proper turf shoes only.

HOCKEY GROUND • Turf ground was inaugurated at Gymkhana on 12th June 2024. • Flood lights are provided to this ground. • Washrooms and changing rooms are available to the users. • This is an irrigated turf, and water sprinklers are provided in the ground.

ATHLETICS TRACK • A synthetic athletic track with 4 lanes is available at Gymkhana (just around the cricket ground area). • flood lights are provided. • The tracks are marked with distance.

AMBULANCE FACILITY @ GYMKHANA Ambulance vehicle will be stationed in the main Gymkhana area on all the working days between 4 pm to 8 pm. One attender from health center will always be available in the vehicle.

VOLLEYBALL COURT • The facility is made available at KV IISc ground, and it has been provided with lighting and a proper clay surface.
